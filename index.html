Who we are!?

We, are a team, an agenda, a drive of employment, that is here to bridge the ones who need jobs to the ones who have jobs to offer!

Why we are different?

We are not an organisation that employs unskilled labour, unlike the present strategies we actually aim to increase their self dependence.

Aim behind रोज़गार

The drive of this project is to eliminate the existance of middleman in the unskilled sector so that we can put more control of the job in the hands of job seekers!

Matter of few Clicks

To keep it simple for everyone who visits our site, we have an agenda to generate the possible work opportunities available in our database with minimum effort from the Job Seeker's side! Just keeping it user-friendly as much as we can!

Upgraded Lifestyle

Unemployment in India is a very ginormous stumbling block at our Economic front! Providing more jobs to people, to those who merely make 2 meals a day would improve their living standard in society and of their families as well!

End to Scrutiny

We aim at providing jobs to those whose suffer on the daily basis, on footpaths, streets, bus stands and want them to connect to those who actually have work for them!

Value Addition

An employed person, who is getting paid for what he does and whose family depends on him, surely adds value to the living of his family and value of his own in society economically as well as socially!
